Example 4: Article Content Structure

Proper heading hierarchy and blockquote within a blog post.

<article>
    <h1>Main Article Title (only one h1)</h1>
    <p>Introduction paragraph...</p>

    <h2>Section Heading (h2)</h2>
    <p>Section content...</p>

    <h3>Subsection (h3)</h3>
    <p>Subsection content...</p>

    <blockquote style="border-left: 4px solid #2563eb; background: #eef2ff; padding: 15px 25px; margin: 20px 0; border-radius: 0 8px 8px 0; font-style: italic;">
        "A meaningful quote from the article."
    </blockquote>
</article>
Rendered Output:

Introduction paragraph lorem ipsum dolor sit amet...

Section Heading

Section content with more details about the topic...

Subsection

Subsection content goes here...

"A meaningful quote from the article."
