Module 9: HTML Quotations — Examples

Example 1: Inline Quotation with <q>

<p>Marie Curie once said, <q>Nothing in life is to be feared, it is only to be understood.</q></p>

<p>She asked, <q cite="https://example.com/interview">Have you read the report?</q></p>
Rendered Output:

Marie Curie once said, Nothing in life is to be feared, it is only to be understood.

She asked, Have you read the report?

Example 2: Nested Quotations

<p>He reported, <q>The witness stated, <q>I saw the car turn left at the intersection</q>, and then the officer took notes.</q></p>
Rendered Output (browsers alternate quote styles):

He reported, The witness stated, I saw the car turn left at the intersection, and then the officer took notes.

Example 3: Block Quotation with <blockquote>

<blockquote cite="https://example.com/speech">
    <p>The only way to do great work is to love what you do. If you haven't found it yet, keep looking. Don't settle.</p>
    <cite>— Steve Jobs, Stanford Commencement Speech</cite>
</blockquote>
Rendered Output:

The only way to do great work is to love what you do. If you haven't found it yet, keep looking. Don't settle.

— Steve Jobs, Stanford Commencement Speech

Example 4: Citations with <cite>

<p>My favorite novel is <cite>To Kill a Mockingbird</cite> by Harper Lee.</p>
<p>The article was published in <cite>Nature Magazine</cite>.</p>
<p>Watch the documentary <cite>The Social Dilemma</cite> on Netflix.</p>
Rendered Output:

My favorite novel is To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee.

The article was published in Nature Magazine.

Watch the documentary The Social Dilemma on Netflix.

Example 5: Abbreviations with <abbr>

<p>The <abbr title="World Wide Web Consortium">W3C</abbr> maintains HTML standards.</p>
<p>Please send your <abbr title="Curriculum Vitae">CV</abbr> by Friday.</p>
<p><abbr title="Doctor">Dr.</abbr> Smith will see you now.</p>
Rendered Output (hover to see tooltip):

The W3C maintains HTML standards.

Please send your CV by Friday.

Dr. Smith will see you now.

Example 6: Contact Information with <address>

<address>
    Written by <a href="mailto:alice@example.com">Alice Johnson</a><br>
    <strong>Company Name</strong><br>
    456 Business Ave, Suite 200<br>
    San Francisco, CA 94102<br>
    <a href="tel:+15551234567">(555) 123-4567</a>
</address>
Rendered Output:
Written by Alice Johnson
Company Name
456 Business Ave, Suite 200
San Francisco, CA 94102
(555) 123-4567
💡 Tip: Use the cite attribute on both <q> and <blockquote> to provide the source URL for attribution.
