4. Landmarks

Press D (NVDA) or VO+U→Landmarks (VoiceOver) to navigate:

Header/Banner - role="banner" or <header>
Navigation - role="navigation" or <nav>
Main Content - role="main" or <main>
Complementary - role="complementary" or <aside>
Content Info/Footer - role="contentinfo" or <footer>
