Example 5: Ordered List Attributes

type="A" (uppercase letters):

  1. First
  2. Second
  3. Third

type="i" (lowercase Roman):

  1. First
  2. Second
  3. Third

start="5":

  1. Item 5
  2. Item 6

reversed:

  1. Top item
  2. Second item
  3. Third item

value attribute on li:

  1. Item 1
  2. Item 10 (jumped)
  3. Item 11
<ol type="A">...</ol> <ol type="i">...</ol> <ol start="5">...</ol> <ol reversed>...</ol> <li value="10">Item 10</li>
