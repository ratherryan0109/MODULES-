Example 5: FAQ Accordion

Click a question to toggle the answer.

<div style="max-width: 600px;">
    <div style="border-bottom: 1px solid #eee;">
        <div onclick="this.nextElementSibling.style.display = this.nextElementSibling.style.display === 'block' ? 'none' : 'block'" style="padding: 15px 0; font-weight: 600; cursor: pointer;">
            What is included in the free trial?
        </div>
        <div style="display: none; padding: 0 0 15px; color: #666;">
            Full access to Pro plan for 14 days. No credit card required.
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
Rendered Output:
What is included in the free trial?
Full access to Pro plan for 14 days. No credit card required.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel anytime. No cancellation fees.
